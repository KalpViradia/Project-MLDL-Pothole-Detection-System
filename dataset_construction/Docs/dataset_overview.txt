# 🧠 What This Means Practically

Because 30% are small:

If you train at 640:

* It will work.
* But small potholes may be borderline.

If you train at 768:

* Small detection improves.
* Training slows.
* VRAM increases.

If you train at 1024:

* Better small detection.
* Batch size drops significantly.
* Slower training.

So now decision becomes strategic, not emotional.
